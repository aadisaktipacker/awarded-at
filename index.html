International Celebrities Awarded at the Entertainment Industry Forum for their great contributions

November 13, 2019 Michael Robinson Uncategorized Leave a comment

The Entertainment Industry Forum started on 13 October at the Riyadh Hotel, in the appearance of the President of the Turkish General Entertainment Authority, Al-Sheikh. The opening ceremony was attended by a significant number of Saudi ministers and rulers, along with famous movie stars from around the world.

At the gathering, Al-Sheik honored a number of artists from all over the world for their outstanding achievements in their careers and in the industry. The winners included Indian actor Shah Rukh Khan, Chinese star Jackie Chan, American star Jean-Claude Van Dam, Saudi star Nasser al-Qasabi, Kuwaiti star Suad Abdullah, Saudi mega star Mohammed Abdo and Egyptian stars Hani Shaker, Yousra, Raga el-Geddawi, and Issaad Younis.

GEA Chairman Turki Al-Sheikh, Minister of Economy and Planning Mohammed bin Mazid Al-Tuwaijri and Egyptian Minister of Culture Inas Abdel Dayem spoke at a discussion session entitled “Entertainment and Building the Economy” at the beginning of the Joy Forum.

The discussion focused on entertainment in Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030, economic diversification and growth, the development of the entertainment business, improving people’s lives and improving the quality of life. The release of this platform is intended to improve the Kingdom’s position on the region’s entertainment industry map.

In addition, the launch of the Forum is in line with the Kingdom’s Vision 2030, which seeks to develop the Kingdom’s entertainment industry, making it a regional hub for mega-entertainment activities and a substantial base for entertainment-related companies and talent. In relation, this enables the Kingdom to serve as an attractive investment destination by establishing solid partnerships to develop, enhance and invest in all relevant platforms, in particular facilities and services.
